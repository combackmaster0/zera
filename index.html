What's New in v0.1.0

Key Features

01

Improved Hardware Compatibility

Expanded support for a wider range of NFC readers and PC/SC-compatible hardware. Seamless plug-and-play detection with automatic driver configuration for ACR122U and beyond.

02

Advanced Scam Detection

Built-in cryptographic validation flags suspicious or tampered vouchers before any transfer occurs. Real-time nullifier verification protects against double-spend and counterfeit voucher attacks.

03

In-Built AI Support

Integrated AI assistant guides you through every step — from first-time setup to advanced voucher management. Get instant answers, troubleshooting help, and smart suggestions without leaving the app.

Key Capabilities

  • NFC Tag Support — Read and write vouchers to NTAG216 NFC tags
  • Drag & Drop Interface — Intuitive workflow for organizing and transferring vouchers
  • Multiple Reader Support — Works with ACR122U and other PC/SC-compatible NFC readers
  • Fast Operations — Optimized read/write operations for efficient transfers
  • Voucher Management — View detailed information and manage your voucher collection

Getting Started

  1. 1Connect your NFC reader to your Windows PC
  2. 2Extract the ZIP archive and launch Zera Desktop
  3. 3Drag vouchers to the write zone to transfer to NFC tags
  4. 4Use the read function to retrieve vouchers from tags

System Requirements

  • Windows 10 or 11 (64-bit)
  • Compatible NFC reader (ACR122U recommended)
  • NTAG216 NFC tags
