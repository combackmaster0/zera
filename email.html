What is New in v0.1.0

Improved Hardware Compatibility

Expanded support for a wider range of NFC readers and PC/SC compatible hardware. Plug in your ACR122U or similar reader and the app detects it automatically, no extra configuration needed.

Advanced Scam Detection

Every voucher is cryptographically validated before any transfer takes place. The app checks nullifiers in real time to catch tampered, duplicated or counterfeit vouchers before they ever touch your NFC tag.

In-Built AI Support

A built-in AI assistant is available right inside the app. Ask it anything from first-time setup questions to advanced voucher workflows and it will walk you through it clearly, without needing to open a browser or search a docs page.

Getting Started

1

Connect your NFC reader to your Windows PC via USB. The ACR122U is recommended and works out of the box.

2

Download and install the MSI package from zeradesktop.app. Run the installer and follow the setup wizard.

3

Drag your vouchers to the write zone and tap your NTAG216 NFC tag to transfer them. It really is that simple.

4

Use the read function to retrieve vouchers from any NTAG216 tag and bring them back into your collection.

 

System Requirements

Operating System Windows 10 or Windows 11 (64-bit)
NFC Reader ACR122U or any PC/SC compatible reader
NFC Tags NTAG216
